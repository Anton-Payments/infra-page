Terraform owns this entire layer. Backend services target GKE NEGs. The Autoneg controller automatically registers and deregisters pod endpoints as they scale. Each subdomain has its own Cloud Armor security policy attached to its backend service. Adaptive Protection is enabled on all policies — Google's ML learns traffic baselines and alerts on anomalies. All policies deploy in preview mode first, then enforce after validation.
